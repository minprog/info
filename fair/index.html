Programming Course
Fair 2024

Amsterdam Science Park, 15 May, 15:00–18:00

Would you like to know more about our courses and minors? Join us at the Programming Course Fair 2024 and connect to teachers and current students.

During the fair you can attend one or more sessions where teachers tell about their programs and courses. There will also be plenty of opportunity to ask questions.

Attending the fair are:

  • Minor Artificial Intelligence (Dutch only)
  • Minor Computational Science (English)
  • Minor Programmeren (Dutch only)
  • Scientific Programming Electives (English)
  • Artificial Intelligence Electives (English and Dutch)
